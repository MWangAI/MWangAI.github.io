About Me

I am currently a research associate with the Western University directed by Prof. Abdelhamid Tayebi. received my B.Sc. in Control Science and Engineering from Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST, China) under the supervision of Prof. Housheng Su in 2013, my M.Sc. in Control Engineering from Lakehead University (LU, Canada) under the supervision of Prof. Abdelhamid Tayebi in 2015, and my Ph.D. in Robotic and Control from Western University (UWO, Canada) under the supervision of Prof. Abdelhamid Tayebi in 2020.

Research Interests

My current research interests are in the areas of nonlinear estimation and control with applications to autonomous robotic systems including

  • State estimation for visual-inertial navigation systems and SLAM

  • Geometric control of autonomous aerial vehicles

  • Multi-agent systems
