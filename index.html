About Me

I am currently a postdoc associate with Prof. Abdelhamid Tayebi at Western University. I received my B.Sc. in Control Science and Engineering from Huazhong University of Science and Technology, China, under the supervision of Prof. Housheng Su in 2013, my M.Sc. in Control Engineering from Lakehead University, Canada, under the supervision of Prof. Abdelhamid Tayebi in 2015, and my Ph.D. in Robotic and Control from Western University, Canada, under the supervision of Prof. Abdelhamid Tayebi in 2020.

Research Interests

My current research interests are in the areas of nonlinear estimation and control with applications to autonomous robotic systems including

  • Robot navigation and SLAM

  • Geometric control of autonomous aerial vehicles

  • Coordination of multi-robot systems
