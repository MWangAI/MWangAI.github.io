About Me

I am currently a Postdoctoral Fellow with the Department of Electrical Engineering at Lakehead University, under the supervision of Prof. Abdelhamid Tayebi.

  • Postdoctoral Associate, (2020-2021), Western University, Canada.

  • Ph.D., Robotics and Control Engineering, (2016-2020), Western University, Canada.

  • M.Sc., Control Engineering, (2014-2015), Lakehead University, Canada.

  • B.Eng., Control Science and Engineering, (2009-2013), Huazhong University of Science and Technology, China.

Research Interests

My research interests mainly focus on nonlinear estimation and control with applications to autonomous robotic systems:

  • Nonlinear state estimation for inertial navigation systems (INS)

  • Geometric control of unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs)

  • Robot navigation and obstacle avoidance

  • Coordination of multi-robot systems
